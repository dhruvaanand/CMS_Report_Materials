DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

Both T2_IT_Bari and T2_IT_Legnaro showing near-zero job counts since ~09:30 UTC.
WMStats shows:
  T2_IT_Bari:    Running: 12   (normal: ~800)
  T2_IT_Legnaro: Running: 4    (normal: ~600)

CRIC shows both sites as Active with normal capacity. No GGUS tickets filed.
Input data is present at both sites. WMAgents for both sites appear healthy
and in Running state.

HTCondor queue at both sites shows jobs in Idle state but not starting -
pilots are either not landing or not matching. This looks like a GlideinWMS
issue rather than a WMAgent issue.

  T2_IT_Bari:    Idle: 3,421  Running: 12  (pilots not matching jobs)
  T2_IT_Legnaro: Idle: 2,890  Running: 4

This means pilots ARE landing (otherwise there would be no jobs in the queue
at all) but the pilots and jobs are not matching. This is different from
pilots not landing at all.

Checked pilot ClassAds. Pilots at both sites are advertising:
  CMSPlatform = "el7"
  
But the queued jobs require:
  CMSPlatform = "el8" or "el9"

The sites are running EL7 (Red Hat Enterprise Linux 7) pilots but our
workflows now require EL8 or EL9. This is a CMSSW compatibility issue -
newer CMSSW releases no longer support EL7.

================================================================================
RESOLUTION
================================================================================

Root Cause:
  T2_IT_Bari and T2_IT_Legnaro still running EL7 OS on majority of worker nodes.
  CMS production workflows now require EL8 or EL9 (newer CMSSW compatibility).
  HTCondor pilot/job ClassAd mismatch resulted in pilots landing but not
  matching any queued jobs, causing effective job starvation despite sites
  appearing operational.

Impact:
  ~1,400 CPU slots effectively unavailable for ~7 hours.
  Multiple Monte Carlo production workflows experienced reduced throughput.
  No job failures, no data loss.

Fix Applied:
  Sites prioritized EL8 node migration. ~300 additional EL8 nodes at T2_IT_Bari
  brought online within 4 hours of ticket creation.

Time to Resolution: 5h 26m (to acceptable throughput)

Follow-up:
  - GGUS-158341 tracking full EL7->EL8 migration at both sites
  - CompOps to monitor both sites daily until migration confirmed complete
  - Propose adding OS platform compatibility to site readiness dashboard
